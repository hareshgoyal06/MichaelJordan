Introduction

Michael Jeffrey Jordan, born on February 17, 1963. You don't have to be a basketball fanatic to have heard this name at least once. Michael Jordan, also known as MJ, is one of the most successful, popular, and wealthy athletes in all of professional sports. When you think about some of the world’s best athletes, you can’t help but think of Michael Jordan almost spontaneously. He’s a phenomenal athlete with unique skills of speed, power, artistry, and competitive desire. One of his famous quotes is, “I always believed that if you put in the work, the results will come in.” He worked for each and everything he achieved; and his game was a direct testament to it. He revolutionized basketball. He made the number 23 infamous in all of sports. He has impacted the fashion industry. He makes breaking headlines for simply appearing. What can’t he do? Michael Jordan will go down in history, as not simply just an athlete, but a trendsetter; someone with influence in all aspects. Having long retired from playing professional sports, he is now a very wealthy businessman, being the principal owner and chairperson of the Charlotte Hornets in the NBA, in addition to owning 23XI Racing in the NASCAR Cup Series. Within this website, we will explore multiple aspects of Michael Jordan, ranging from his basketball career (including some looming controversy), to his basketball/fashion influence, to even his stats.
